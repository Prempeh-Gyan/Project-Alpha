Frequently Asked Questions


  • Indefinite Residence Permit

    I am married to a Ghanaian and lived in Ghana for 3 years. Can I apply for the Indefinite Residence Permit?


    Reviewed about a year ago

    Prior to your application, you should have lived in Ghana throughout for 12 months, preceeding that for an aggregate of 5 years within a 7 year period.

  • Indefinite Residence Permit

    I am on a visitor's visa and keep renewing it. Can I apply for the indefinite residence permit?


    Reviewed about a year ago

    No. You should have a valid residence permit before applying for the indefinite permit.

  • Indefinite Residence Permit

    Does my indefinite residence permit expire?


    Reviewed about a year ago

    Yes. If you leave Ghana for a period of 12 months and more, you have to reapply.

  • Indefinite Residence Permit

    What are the benefits of an indefinite residence permit?


    Reviewed about a year ago

    You are entitled to live and work in Ghana, enter without a visa and your dependant child is eligible for a dependant's permit.
