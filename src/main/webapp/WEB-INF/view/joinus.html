Welcome To ISAG | Join Us!

ISAG is here to help newcomers, to exchange our experiences as international spouses, to become friends, and to become family.

Join us and benefit from a culturally diverse and strong community that is committed to enriching our lives and creating a positive experience!
As an ISAG member you can:

  • Mingle with people with similar backgrounds and experiences;
  • Learn and share with a multifaceted group;
  • Build and strengthen your network and friendships;
  • Be united in the fact that you are married to a Ghanaian spouse;
  • Become actively involved in helping shape the future of other Ghanaian spouses;

We would like to meet you and welcome you. Sign up, join us and enjoy being part of this multi-faceted family!
