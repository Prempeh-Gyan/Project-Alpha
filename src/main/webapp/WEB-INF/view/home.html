  • Who Are We

    ISAG is a self-help association that was formed in 1994 to assist and support foreign spouses of Ghanaians in their integration into Ghanaian society. Its unique character derives from the diverse cultures of our members, their belief in the value of this diversity, and their commitment to Ghana.

  • What We Do

    ISAG acts as a support system for foreign spouses and their Ghanaian partners and families, enhancing its members’ ability to maintain their commitments to Ghana, sustain their partnership with their spouses, fulfill their custodianship of their children, and in other ways to participate fully in the social, cultural and economic life of Ghana.

  • Our Milestones

    ISAG successfully lobbied to achieve the introduction, by the government, of the Indefinite Residence Permit for foreign spouses. We also achieved an exemption for Ghanaian spouses from the foreign investment criteria requirements in the GIPC legislation, making it feasible for Ghanaian spouses to establish their businesses in Ghana.

What We Do...

  • AS FOREIGN SPOUSES

    We advocate and promote the full legal rights of our members to live and work as Ghanaian spouses do. We are members of Ghanaian families and therefore uphold the importance of the family in the full development of the individual and the nation.

    AS PARENTS OF GHANAIAN CHILDREN

    We assist our members in their special responsibility of nurturing citizens of dual heritage. We offer the additional support that a member’s family would normally provide as we are individuals away from their native homes.
